Geometry Chaos Editor Help

Menu bar

Menu bar at the top allows you to save and load your map and to add music to your map.

As the editor doesn't have Undo feature, it is recommended to frequently save your map to have the ability to rollback just in case.

Editor accepts music in wav format. It is not tested with other formats and most probably will not work. When you added music to the editor, it will be saved to the map on the next save.
